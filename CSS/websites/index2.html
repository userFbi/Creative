My Trip to Ratnagiri & Chiplun

Trip Dates: 14th June - 18th June

14th June: Departure & Arrival

Train departure at 8:00 PM. Arrive at Ratnagiri station by 6:00 AM.

14th June: Ratnagiri to Chiplun

Take another train from Ratnagiri to Chiplun. Stay at my friend's house.

15th June: Rest & Explore Village

Relax and explore the village around Chiplun.

16th June: Nageshwar Trek

Go for the Nageshwar trek in Chiplun.

17th June: Ganpatiphule & Beaches

Visit Ganpatiphule Temple, then Aare Ware Beach, followed by Kasheli Beach.

18th June: Explore Ratnagiri & Return

Explore Ratnagiri places during the day. Evening train back home at 8:00 PM.
